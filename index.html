Welcome, I'm glad you're here!

Hello! Welcome to my portfolio page. My name is Anna Everly, and I am a third year computational media student at Georgia Institute of Technology. My mission is to merge art and computing by implementing new technologies in an innovative, creative way to tell stories that connect with people.
I've always had a passion for telling stories and art in any form, whether that be music, films, books, theatre, dance, visual arts, anything. Art is how I am able to relate to the world and it's what keeps me going; it's a way for us all to relate to each other. That being said, we live in the digital age where technology is rapidly advancing and creating never before possible opportunities. I want to incorporate both disciplines in developing new ways to tell stories and connect people.

I am an artist who loves stories and is seeking innovative uses of technology. This page exists to highlight my personal, academic, and professional projects which pertain to computing, media, and art. Here you will find samples of my music, writing, photography, and computational media work, along with some personal information about my story.
