Hi! My name is am Anna Everly. I am originally from Savannah, GA, but I am studying in Atlanta, GA right now and would love to continue living in a big city in the next stage of my life. Growing up, I was the oldest of five siblings and participated in a variety of artistic activities, including many theater productions, managing my own piano studio, and even attempting to create my own animated movie. I love all art for different reasons, but my biggest passion has always been piano. I've taken lessons for more of my life than not, taught my own students, performed in numerous concerts, and dabbled in composing. I attended a performing and visual arts high school where I was able to further my craft and also had the opportunity to be exposed to so many different kinds of art. I developed an appreciation for art in every form and its vast ability to convey emotion and tell stories. That is why I am here: to meld my passion (art) with practicality (technology) to create and connect.
