Computational Media

Computational media is the intersection of technology and art. I don't have many completed computational media projects to showcase yet. However, I am in the process of developing them! Through the computational media degree program I am completing, I am learning a variety of useful skills, including building websites, graphic design basics, and much more. I have experience in multiple coding languages, including Python, Java, JavaScript, and HTML/CSS. In addition to building this website, other projects I have worked on include recreating a scene in A-Frame, coding a Twitter bot, and creating a Georgia Tech-themed cookie-clicker game. I'm looking forward to adding to these projects, so stay tuned for more updates!
