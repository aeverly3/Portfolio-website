Piano

Piano has always been an integral part of my life. I've taken lessons since kindergarten and was a piano major at my performing arts high school. I have participated in countless performances, and one of proudest achievements was my solo senior recital in March 2020. This is where my connection to art first emerged, and after the probably thousands of hours I've poured into it, piano remains very close to my heart. Below are a few samples of me expressing my art with some performances through the years.
